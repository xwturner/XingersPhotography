About Me



My name is Xinghua Turner.

Welcome!

A little bit about me, I grew up in Eagle River, Alaska, and am always blessed to call this place my home. I picked up photography when I was in my early teens. Now I would consider myself a professional amateur photographer. I know more than the beginning person out there, but a little less than a professional. My passion has grown throughout the years. My field of interest embraces landscapes with an emphasis on nature.

In my spare time you can find me at the ice skating rink, hiking, or traveling. I am always looking for new adventures and places to go. Feel free to reach out and contact me!
